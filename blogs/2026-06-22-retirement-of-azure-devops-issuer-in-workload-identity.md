The Azure DevOps issuer in workload identity federation service connections is being deprecated, with retirement planned for July 1, 2027, standardizing on the Microsoft Entra issuer across Azure services. Existing connections continue functioning until retirement, while new connections have used the Microsoft Entra issuer by default since November 2025.
